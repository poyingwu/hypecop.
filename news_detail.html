In order to celebrate the 60th birthday of its representative, Kentaro Higuchi, the fashionable street Meisho JB Voice, which has many stores in Aichi and Gifu prefectures, was riding on by Obana Daisuke and Nankiyoshi, who are well-connected with them. At this moment, through the new project "JACK in THE RED" launched by JB Voice at the beginning of last month, a new joint project between N.HOOLYWOOD and Graphpaper was launched.

With the theme of "08⑦3⑦3", the two parties brought products such as Reversible Flight Jacket and Jeans, which are designs that have never appeared before for both brands. The sale is also limited to JACK in the BOX, SP., and ARGUS MP stores. The flight jacket, natural color, and washed denim trousers are priced at ¥63,800, ¥31,900, and ¥37,400, respectively. On sale today, Obana Daisuke and Nan Kiyuki will also visit the JACK in the BOX store to build momentum. Interested readers may wish to pay more attention.

The CDG x Vault by Vans OG Authentic LX, first introduced in October of 2018, and Vans OG Old Skool LX, released earlier this year, are now being offered exclusively on Dover Street Market.

The CDG Vans Authentic is dressed in a black and white color scheme with “CDG” branding spread across the entirety of the uppers and a large rubber “CDG” logo resting parallel with the laces. A rubber sole unit below integrating a white Vans logo tag on the rear completes the pairs.

The CDG Vans Old Skool features untouched two-tone suede and canvas uppers complete with white contrast stitching. CDG graphics are incorporated below stretching from the midfoot to the heel, stopping abruptly at the textured toe bumper. Traditional waffle outsoles round out the look. The two pairs are now available exclusively at Dover Street Market locations worldwide.
